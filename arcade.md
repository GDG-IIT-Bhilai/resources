# 🕹️ Google Cloud Arcade & Facilitator Program: Evergreen Guide

Welcome to the ultimate playground for learning cloud architecture through hands-on gameplay! The Google Cloud Arcade is a free, self-paced training program built to help you master core cloud engineering concepts, earn official skill badges, accumulate bonus points, and claim premium developer merchandise.

---

## 🚀 How the Arcade Works (The Step-by-Step Flow)

The program runs on a simple, continuous loop: **Subscribe & Enroll ➡️ Play Labs ➡️ Earn Badges ➡️ Accumulate Milestone/Bonus Points ➡️ Redeem Premium Swag.**

### 1. Set Up Your Profiles & Whitelist
Before you can play, you need to properly configure your identity ecosystem:
* **The Arcade Console:** This tracks your overall game progress, accumulated points, and seasonal player achievements.
* **Google Cloud Skills Boost (Qwiklabs):** This is the active lab environment where you spin up virtual machines, configure networks, and run backend logic.
* **Essential Whitelisting:** You must add `googlecloudedu-noreply@google.com` directly to your email contacts list or safe-sender filter. This ensures your mandatory credits pass and enrollment setup do not get blocked by Spam or Promotions algorithms
> *Note: Make sure to use the exact same email address across all profiles so your badges and automated credit syncing work flawlessly!*

### 2. Play Monthly Games & Trivia
Every month, the Arcade drops limited-seat tracks that you should complete first before they expire:
* **Arcade Games & Simulator:** Immersive tracks spanning Arcade Base Camp, Adventure, Voyage, Trail, and advanced architecture simulators.
* **Trivia Sprints:** Focused weekly challenge labs dedicated to specific Google Cloud domains, system features, or technical milestones.

### 3. Earn Digital Skill Badges
Progressing through challenges puts you on track to clear full credentials. Every time you complete a targeted sequence of labs, an official **Google Cloud Skill Badge** is minted directly onto your public Skills Boost profile as verifiable proof of your technical capabilities. You must explicitly enroll in these skill badges on the platform to ensure they register toward your point milestones.

## 🎁 The Milestone Points System & Facilitator Campaign

This is what makes the Arcade incredibly popular among college students. Completing regular tracks and syncing them with the **Arcade Facilitator Program** allows you to accumulate heavy bonus multipliers.

### How Points Accumulate
* **Arcade Games:** Every unique game or trivia badge you earn equals **1 Arcade Point**.
* **Skill Badges:** Earning individual badges is not enough; you must clear them in pairs. Every **2 Skill Badges** you earn equal **1 Arcade Point**.
* **Campaign Bonus Points:** By joining the Facilitator program cohort, completing specific quotas unlocks massive flat bonus point drop tiers. You only receive bonus points for the highest single milestone you clear.

### The Facilitator Milestone Tiers
* 🏆 **Milestone 1:** Complete 6 Arcade Games & 18 Skill Badges = 15 Arcade Points + **5 Bonus Points** *(Eligible for +10 extra points via the Industry-Ready Bonus Milestone)*.
* 🏆 **Milestone 2:** Complete 8 Arcade Games & 34 Skill Badges = 25 Arcade Points + **15 Bonus Points** *(Eligible for +10 extra points via the Industry-Ready Bonus Milestone)*.
* 🏆 **Milestone 3:** Complete 10 Arcade Games & 50 Skill Badges = 35 Arcade Points + **25 Bonus Points** *(Eligible for +10 extra points via the Industry-Ready Bonus Milestone)*.
* 🏆 **Ultimate Milestone:** Complete 12 Arcade Games & 66 Skill Badges = 45 Arcade Points + **35 Bonus Points** *(Eligible for +10 extra points via the Industry-Ready Bonus Milestone)*.

## 🎒 The Prize Counter & Player Achievements

Your accumulated points (Arcade + Bonus) are tracked via the monthly *Arcade Insider* newsletter and used to lock in seasonal Player Achievements. Points are non-transferable, stick strictly to your personal learning record, and carry a **6-month expiration rule**.

### 🚨 Critical Mechanics: First-Come Caps & Weekly Spot Refreshes
High-tier player allocations are strictly capped. Available slots across these tiers **refresh dynamically on a weekly basis**. If an achievement tier shows 0% availability, watch the global counter closely and secure your claim the split-second the spots reset.

* **Arcade Trooper (50 Points):** Unlocks base-tier foundational learning gear.
* **Arcade Ranger (75 Points):** Unlocks intermediate customized track swag.
* **Arcade Champion (95 Points):** Unlocks premium developer asset tier merchandise.
* **Arcade Legend (120 Points):** Unlocks elite, heavy-duty tier developer assets.

## 🎯 Pro-Tips for Freshers Starting Out

* **Watch the Timer:** Qwiklabs environments provide temporary, real-time cloud credentials that self-destruct when the countdown hits zero. Read the manual steps before clicking "Start Lab".
* **Claim Your Free Credits Pass:** Keep an eye on the official GDG IIT Bhilai communication channels! Valid facilitator enrollment provides a 24-48 hour window to claim an unrestricted credits pass so you never have to pay out of pocket to complete labs.
* **Never Cheat the Checker:** Using automated execution scripts, scraper macros, or rapid copy-paste cheats results in an **immediate, permanent ban** from the Arcade and all future Google Developer Program opportunities. 
* **Fixing reCAPTCHA Registration Roadblocks:** If the signup security prompt hangs or fails, clear your extensions, open a clean **Incognito Window**, click the confirmation button **strictly once**, and allow 3–5 seconds for the authorization handshake to clear.
