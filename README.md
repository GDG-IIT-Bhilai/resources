# 📚 GDG IIT Bhilai — Technical Resources & Learning Hub

Welcome to the central resource repository for Google Developer Groups (GDG) IIT Bhilai! This hub is designed to simplify your developer journey by providing curated learning paths, official program blueprints, and hands-on resource sheets for Google technologies.

Whether you are a fresher exploring your first lines of code, a cloud enthusiast chasing student campaigns, or an advanced developer prepping for industry-grade professional certifications, explore our sub-directories below to get started.

---

## 📁 Repository Directory

Navigate through our dedicated program guides to lock in your next technical milestones:

### 🕹️ [Google Cloud Arcade & Facilitator Guide](./arcade.md)
* **What it is:** Our comprehensive student roadmap for the official Google Cloud Skills Arcade and the active 2026 Facilitator campaign.
* **What you'll find:** Step-by-step onboarding sequences, exact point accumulation mechanics (Regular vs. Bonus tracking), mandatory email whitelisting workflows, and instructions to claim your seasonal developer swag.
* **Best for:** Freshers and students looking to learn cloud engineering, data mesh layouts, and AI infrastructure via gamified, zero-cost lab simulation tracks.

### 🎓 [Google Cloud Certifications & GEAR Blueprint](./Get_Certified.md)
* **What it is:** The official candidate handbook for the Gemini Enterprise Agent Ready (GEAR) "Get Certified" program.
* **What you'll find:** Structural outlines for rolling application cohorts (Editions 1, 2, and 3), criteria to capture fully funded examination vouchers, and detailed deep-dives into supported certification tracks including Associate Cloud Engineer (ACE), Professional Cloud Architect (PCA), and Generative AI Leader (GAIL).
* **Best for:** Dedicated developers and engineering students preparing to validate industry-grade expertise on a global scale to accelerate internship and placement opportunities.

## 🎯 Getting Started Checklist for Freshers

1. **Join the Community:** Ensure you are added to our official camp communication channels to receive active access keys, voucher announcements, and monthly credit drops.
2. **Setup Your Profile:** Head to the respective guides above to link your public Google Developer profile correctly before joining live lab sessions.
3. **Follow the Rules:** Review the community integrity and tracking telemetry rules highlighted inside each document to ensure your milestone progress registers safely toward prize allocations.
